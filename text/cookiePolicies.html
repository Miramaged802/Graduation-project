What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, mobile, or tablet) by the websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide important functionalities, as well as to collect information about your browsing activities.

Cookies can be either "session cookies" that are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent cookies" that remain on your device for a longer period of time. Cookies can also be categorized as "first-party cookies" (set by the website you're visiting) or "third-party cookies" (set by other websites or services).

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to choose whether to accept or reject cookies on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control and delete cookies through the browser settings. You can usually find the cookie settings in the "Options," "Tools," or "Preferences" menu of your web browser.
  • Cookie Management Tools: You can use various cookie management tools and browser extensions/add-ons to help you control and monitor the cookies on our website. These tools can help you understand what cookies are being set and allow you to accept or reject them more easily.
  • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner that allows you to customize your cookie preferences and opt-out of certain categories of cookies.

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some features and functionalities of our website may not work as intended, and your user experience may be affected.
