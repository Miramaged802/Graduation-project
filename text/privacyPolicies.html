Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving Our Services: We use your information to deliver, maintain, and enhance the antiques and handmade crafts marketplace, customer support, delivery services, and expert advisor offerings, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable user experience.
  • Facilitating Transactions: Your personal and financial information is used to process payments, handle shipping and returns, and fulfill orders, enabling secure and efficient transactions on our platform.
  • Personalization and Recommendations: We analyze your preferences, interests, and usage patterns to personalize your experience, provide relevant content and product recommendations, and deliver tailored offers and promotions.
  • Communication and Account Management: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, transactions, and any updates or changes to our services. This includes sending important notices, responding to your inquiries, and providing customer support.
  • Fraud Prevention and Compliance: We utilize your information to detect and prevent fraudulent activities, enforce our policies and terms of service, and ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations in Egypt.

How We Protect Your Information

Safeguarding the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal information is of the utmost importance to us. We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption: We encrypt your personal and financial information during transmission and storage using industry-standard encryption protocols, such as SSL/TLS and AES, to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your information to only those employees and authorized personnel who need it to perform their duties. We also implement stringent access policies and procedures to ensure the appropriate use of your data.
  • Monitoring and Incident Response: We continuously monitor our systems and networks for any suspicious activity or unauthorized access attempts. In the event of a security incident, we have established incident response and data breach notification protocols to mitigate the impact and notify affected users promptly.
  • Regular Security Reviews: We regularly review and update our security practices, technologies, and policies to stay ahead of evolving cybersecurity threats and comply with the latest data protection regulations in Egypt.

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment processors, shipping carriers, analytics providers, and customer support partners, who assist us in operating and improving our platform.

We are committed to providing you with transparency and control over your personal information. As our user, you have the following rights and choices:

  • Access and Review: You can access and review the personal information we have stored in your account by logging in and visiting your account settings.
